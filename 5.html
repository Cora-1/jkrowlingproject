Harry Potter and the Goblet Of Fire
The rules of the Triwizard Tournament, which is about to take place at Hogwarts, only allow wizards over the age of seventeen to enter. So Harry can only daydream about winning.Then, to his surprise, on Hallowe’en when the Goblet of Fire makes its selection, his name is picked out of the magical cup.Harry will face life-endangering tasks, dragons and Dark wizards.He’ll have to rely on the help of his friends if he is to make it through the contest alive.
